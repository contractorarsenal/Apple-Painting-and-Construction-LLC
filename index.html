Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Answers before you call. Still have questions? Marco is happy to walk through your project anytime.

What services does Apple Painting and Construction offer?
We offer a full range of painting and construction services including interior and exterior painting, cabinet painting, deck and fence staining, drywall installation and repair, remodeling, new construction, and commercial painting. If your project involves paint, drywall, or building, Apple can handle it from start to finish.
How long does an interior painting project take?
It depends on the size of the project. A single room can often be completed in a day. A full home interior typically takes 3 to 5 days depending on prep work, number of rooms, and finish details. Marco will give you a realistic timeline during your free estimate.
Do you provide free estimates?
Yes, always. Marco comes to your property, walks the project with you, and gives you a written estimate with no obligation. No service call fees, no surprise costs. You'll know exactly what you're getting before any work begins.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. Apple Painting and Construction LLC is fully licensed and insured in Oregon. You're protected on every job. We carry general liability and our crew is covered so you have zero exposure on your property.
Do you do commercial painting jobs?
Yes. Apple works with commercial clients including offices, retail spaces, warehouses, and multi-unit properties. We schedule around your business hours and deliver clean, professional results without disrupting your operation.
